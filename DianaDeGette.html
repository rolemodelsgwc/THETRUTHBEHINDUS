About Her

Ethnicity: American

Birthday: July 29, 1957

Born in: Tachikawa, Tokyo, Japan

Occupation

Diana Louise DeGette is the U.S. Representative for Colorado's 1st congressional district, serving since 1997, and a Chief Deputy Whip. She is a member of the Democratic Party. The district is based in Denver.

Achievements

In 2012, DeGette won reelection against Republican nominee Danny Stroud, Libertarian nominee Frank Atwood and Green Party nominee Gary Swing. DeGette won with 68.23% of the votes.
