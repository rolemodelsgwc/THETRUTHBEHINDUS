About Her

Ethnicity: African-American

Birthday: November 25, 1957

Education: University of Colorado, Duke University

Occupation

Lisa Michelle Borders is the president of the WNBA, and the former Vice President of Global Community Affairs at The Coca-Cola Company. She became the fourth president of the WNBA on February 10, 2016.

Achievements

In 2010, Lisa became a founding leader of No Labels, a citizen's movement of Republicans, Democrats and Independents, whose mission is to address the politics of problem solving.
