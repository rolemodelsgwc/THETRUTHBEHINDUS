About Her

Ethnicity: American

Birthday: August 6, 1911

Born in: Jamestown, NY

Died in: April 26, 1989

Occupation

Lucille Désirée Ball was an American actress, comedienne, model, film-studio executive, and producer. She was best known as the star of the self-produced sitcoms I Love Lucy, The Lucy–Desi Comedy Hour, The Lucy Show, Here's Lucy, and Life with Lucy.

Achievements

Lucille Ball had won the awards Kennedy Center Honors, Presidential Medal of Freedom,Golden Globe Cecil B. DeMille Award, Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Comedy Series,TV Land Legacy of Laughter Award, TCA Career Achievement Award, Primetime Emmy Award for Best Actress - Continuing Performance, and the Golden Globe Award for Television Achievement
