About Her

Ethnicity: American

Birthday: January 26, 1958

Born in: Metairie, Louisiana, U.S.

Occupation

Ellen DeGeneres is an American tv host, comedian , writer, and producer. She had starred in a popular sitcom called Ellen from the years 1994-1998. She had appeared in the famous childhood movies Finding Nemo and Finding Dory.

Achievements

Ellen DeGeneres has accomplished many achievements throughout her career. She has earned the the Presidential award of Freedom, Teen Choice Awards for Comedy, People’s Award for Favorite Comedian, and etc.
