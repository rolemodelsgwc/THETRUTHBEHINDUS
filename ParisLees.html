About Her

Ethnicity: British

Born in: Hucknall, United Kingdom

Occupation

Paris Lees is a journalist, presenter, and transgender rights activist.Lees founded the first British magazine aimed at the trans community, META,and was the acting assistant editor of Gay Times.She also has columns in both Gay Times and Diva, and was the first trans cover girl for Diva.

Achievements

Paris had topped the Independent on Sunday's 2013 Pink List, came second in the 2014 Rainbow List, and was awarded the Positive Role Model Award for LGBT in the 2012 National Diversity Awards.
