About Her

Ethnicity: American

Birthday: August 15, 1964

Born in: Dallas, Texas

Occupation

Melinda Ann Gates is an American philanthropist, former Microsoft employee, and wife of Microsoft founder Bill Gates. She is co-founder of the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.

Achievements

Melinda has been awarded the Jefferson Awards, Spanish Prince of Asturias Award for International Cooperation , Insignia of the Order of the Aztec Eagle, honorary degrees from the University of Cambridge, Presidential Medal of Freedom, Legion of Honour, and the Otto Hahn Peace Medal.
