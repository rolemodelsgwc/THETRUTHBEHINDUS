About Her

Ethnicity: Black, White, Native American, Spanish, and Filipino

Birthday: April 24, 1995

Born in: Oakland, California, U.S.

Occupation

Kehlani Ashley Parrish is an American singer, songwriter and dancer who is currently signed to Atlantic Records. Kehlani is originally from Oakland, California where she achieved initial fame as a member of the teen pop group Poplyfe.

Achievements

Kehlani was nominated for the Centric Award and the Best Female R&B/Pop Artist.
